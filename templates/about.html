    About Signova

    Breaking communication barriers through innovation

    Who We Are

    Signova is a Rwandan-born innovation initiative developing advanced sign language recognition and learning technologies to bridge communication gaps between deaf and hearing communities. Founded by engineers, accessibility advocates, and AI specialists, we combine electronics, telecommunications, and computer vision to make communication inclusive, secure, and real-time.

    While our ultimate vision spans multiple global sign languages, our current focus is delivering accurate, privacy-conscious Rwandan Sign Language (RSL) translation and education—empowering communities and creating equal access to information.

    Our Technology Focus

    • Core Development: Real-time RSL gesture recognition powered by deep learning and computer vision.
    • Language Expansion: Progressive addition of African and international sign languages.
    • Learning System: Interactive, accessible tools for sign language learning in schools, communities, and online.
    • Cybersecurity Integration: End-to-end encryption and on-device AI processing to ensure user privacy and safety.

    Current Language Support

    • ✅ Rwandan Sign Language (RSL): Full camera-based translation to text and speech.
    • ✅ English: Text-to-sign conversion for inclusive communication.
    • ✅ Learning Materials: Structured lessons, sign dictionaries, and practice tools for both RSL and international sign languages.

    Key Features

    Real-Time Translation

    • AI-powered RSL interpretation via camera.
    • Text-to-sign conversion with animated avatars.
    • Voice output for sign-to-speech.

    Learning Platform

    • Step-by-step RSL and sign language lessons.
    • Interactive video dictionary with common signs.
    • Practice exercises with instant feedback.
    • Cultural and linguistic context for RSL users.

    Our Mission

    Signova exists to break down communication barriers through engineering innovation and ethical AI. We believe language accessibility is a basic human right—not a privilege—and are committed to building tools that make communication inclusive for everyone, everywhere.

    Our Team

    Signova unites:

    • Deaf community advocates and ASL/RSL linguists
    • AI & computer vision engineers
    • Accessibility design specialists
    • UX researchers with cultural expertise

    30% of our team are deaf or hard-of-hearing, ensuring the technology serves the community authentically.

    Our Technology

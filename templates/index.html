    Signova: Smart Sign Language Gesture Recognition and Vocalization System

    Signova is an innovative hybrid assistive communication system designed to bridge the communication gap for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in Rwanda and beyond. Leveraging both wearable sensor technology and computer vision, Signova translates Rwandan Sign Language (RSL) gestures into audible speech in real-time, enabling seamless interaction with hearing individuals in everyday scenarios.

    Innovative Technology

    Wearable sensor gloves and camera-based vision system working together for precise gesture recognition and real-time vocalization.

    Social Impact

    Real-time translation, specialized for Rwandan Sign Language, hybrid technology, user-friendly interface, and continuous learning.

    A World Without Barriers

    Every day, millions of deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face invisible walls. These barriers limit access to education, job opportunities, and even simple social interactions. In Rwanda and beyond, the challenge is not ability — it's accessibility.

    At Signova, we believe that communication should never depend on whether you can hear or speak. Our vision is simple but powerful: a world where every voice — spoken or signed — is heard and understood.

    Through innovative technology and a deep commitment to inclusivity, we're breaking down these barriers one conversation at a time. Our sign language recognition system doesn't just translate gestures; it bridges worlds, connects communities, and opens doors that were previously closed.
